## Privacy Policy

This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, store and protect the personal information of users on our newsletter website. We are committed to respecting your privacy and protecting the information you share with us. Please read this policy carefully to understand our practices regarding your personal data and how we treat it.

- Collection of Personal Information: <br/>
When you subscribe to our newsletter, we ask you to provide your email address. We only collect personal information that you provide to us. No other personal data is automatically collected.

- Use of personal information: <br/>
We use your email address to send you our newsletter and any other relevant communication that we deem useful to share with you. Your email address will not be used for any other purpose without your express consent.

- Retention of Personal Information:<br/>
We keep your email address in our database for as long as you are subscribed to our newsletter, unless you choose to unsubscribe. If you wish to unsubscribe, please complete the [unsubscribe form](../unsubscribe) with the email address you wish to be removed. We then securely delete your information from our database.

- Protection of personal information: <br/>
We take reasonable steps to protect your personal information from unauthorized use, access or disclosure. However, please note that no method of transmission over the Internet, or method of electronic storage, is completely secure. Therefore, we cannot guarantee the absolute security of transmitted or expanded information.

- Disclosure of personal information to third parties: <br/>
We do not sell, rent or disclose your personal information to third parties unless we are legally required to do so or it is necessary to fulfill our contractual obligations to you.

- Changes to the privacy policy: <br/>
We reserve the right to modify this privacy policy at any time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date listed at the top. It is your responsibility to regularly review this policy to be aware of any changes.

- Consent: <br/>
By subscribing to our newsletter and providing your personal information, you consent to the collection, use and disclosure of your information in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

If you have any questions or concerns regarding this Privacy Policy, please contact us at the following email address: contact.npmbytes@gmail.com.
